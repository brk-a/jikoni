Who We Are

DNM & Associates LLP is one of East Africa's leading firms of accountants, tax professionals, and business advisors, registered and approved by the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya. We deliver quality services covering auditing, assurance, accounting, tax consultancy, and management consulting for a diverse range of clients including family-owned companies, donor-funded organisations, government agencies, SoEs, startups, joint ventures, NGOs and multinationals.

Our Mission

To deliver excellent, timely, and value-for-money professional services to specification and in accordance with the highest standards.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global partner, focused on developing our markets, creating fulfilling experiences for our people, and being a socially responsible business.

Our Values

Quality, Integrity, Independence, Commitment, and Customer Focus drive everything we do. We are committed to maintaining the highest professional standards whilst delivering exceptional value.

What We Do

We offer comprehensive professional services tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses across various industries.

Accounting Services

Financial statement preparation and presentation, bookkeeping outsourcing services, IFRS and IPSAS implementation & compliance reviews, payroll processing, accounting staff training and on-the-job finance coaching, year-end closing procedures, statutory filings (VAT, NSSF, NHIF), and asset tagging & asset register development.

Audit & Assurance

Statutory audit and expenditure verification, internal audit, information security auditing, fraud investigation reports, mergers and acquisitions, financial due diligence reports, stock and asset auditing, risk advisory, and loss control and transaction advisory.

Tax Advisory

Tax due diligence and health checks, dispute resolution with revenue authorities, transfer pricing policy review, cross-border tax advisory, and tax compliance and payroll services.

Business Advisory

Economic feasibility studies, transaction advisory services, CFO services, business valuations, and budgeting, forecasting and projections.

Financial Planning

Comprehensive financial planning and analysis services to support informed decision-making and long-term success.

Compliance Services

Ensuring your business meets all regulatory requirements and maintains compliance with local and international standards.
